Local strata management in Fremantle
and surrounding suburbs

We are a family business with over 30 years of experience dealing in Strata Company Management.

We offer personalised service, together with a wealth of strata management experience, legal and technical knowledge, clerical services and accounting facilities, to assist your Strata Company in making decisions with confidence.

We work with an extensive network of competent outsourced service providers and contractors, who you can rely upon to act with professionalism, honesty and integrity.

Whether it be a community of high-rise apartments, single-story villas, townhouses, or a commercial shopping centre – we are experienced in managing strata schemes of all shapes and sizes, and strive to tailor our services to your needs.

Freo is our territory!

Having lived and worked in Fremantle for the best part of 40 years, our family understand the importance and value in preserving the heritage of our community, and strive to manage and maintain our buildings accordingly.

Being local means we can visit your property regularly as instructed by the council of owners, so we can personally oversee works and meet contractors as required. Life is that much easier when you are just around the corner.

Communication

We take the time to form respectful working relationships with our owners. In being a family-run business, we maintain consistent staff, so you will always know the person on the other end of the line.

Efficiency

Our Strata Managers each manage their own portfolio with the help of a dedicated assistant and support staff. As such, they can act swiftly with a full background knowledge of every strata scheme under their management.

Professionalism

All of our strata managers hold a Certificate IV in Strata Community Management as required under the Strata Titles (General) Regulations 2019, and are fully accredited members of the industry body, the Strata Community Association (WA).

Sustainability

We strive to assist those who are looking to reduce their carbon footprint. By working with your neighbours, you could do this at a far lesser cost and maximise output for everyone. Learn more

Find that you never speak to the same person twice?

You will find the family operated business structure maintains consistent personnel, which augments a more personalised service, and a greater degree of familiarity with the business of your strata company.

Sustainability

At John Dethridge Strata Services, we take environmental responsibility seriously. This is why weve partnered with Greenfleet, a leading environmental non-profit organisation who are taking practical action against climate change by helping to restore Australia’s forests, recreate crucial habitat for native wildlife and transform degraded land back to its natural state.
